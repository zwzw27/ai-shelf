About this benchmark

AI assistants have become a shopping interface: people ask them what to buy, and the answer arrives with the research already done. This benchmark measures whose work that answer is built on. We ask ChatGPT, Gemini and Claude 150 real consumer buying questions across three markets, trace every source they cite, and classify each one: open-web editorial, forums, video, vendor or retailer pages.

The 150 queries Raw dataset Source classifications
Method: official APIs only · 3 runs per query inside one dated week · domains classified by model, spot-checked by hand (agreement rate published) · full pipeline open in this repository. A benchmark of what AI answers are made of — not a ranking of the assistants themselves.
